# Poetrystack

**Restack poetry on Substack.**

Poetrystack adds a floating toolbar whenever you highlight a poetry block on any Substack post. One click opens the notes composer, builds a styled quote card from your selection, and attaches the screenshot automatically - no copying, no screenshotting, no manual formatting.

---

## Install

[**→ Get it on the Chrome Web Store**](https://chromewebstore.google.com)

---

## How it works

1. Open any Substack post with poetry
2. Highlight the lines you want to restack
3. Click **Restack with note** in the floating toolbar
4. Pick a quote card theme
5. Keep the post preview visible alongside the card (this notifies the author)
6. Hit **Post**

You can also click **Copy quote** to copy the selected text straight to your clipboard.

---

## Features

- Detects poetry blocks automatically (`<pre>` tags and poem-classed elements)
- 9 quote card themes - solid colors and blurred image backgrounds
- Left and center text alignment
- Preserves bold and italic formatting from the original poem
- Falls back to clipboard copy if Substack's UI can't be found
- Works on `substack.com` and custom Substack domains
- No data collected, no external servers, no analytics

---

## Privacy

Poetrystack collects nothing. No data leaves your browser. See the full [privacy policy](https://roohafzaa.github.io/poetrystack/privacy.html).
